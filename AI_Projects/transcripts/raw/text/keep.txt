    • Cloudflare Argo: Mask internal services, enforce HTTPS, block bots, and hide true origin.
    • AWS EC2 Jump Server: Minimal intermediary node for secure, credential-gated remote access.
Phase II: Fortress Athena
    • Athena & Hera: Core internal servers handling routing, PXE boot, and encrypted storage.
    • Internal Router: Layered ACLs, VLAN segmentation, and isolated zones for simulation, AI, and services.

Phase III: The Great Library

HIPAA-COMPLIANT BY DESIGN
Privacy isn’t a feature — it’s a foundation. Vector Trust is committed to maintaining full compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), ensuring that all systems handling protected health information (PHI):
    • Use end-to-end encryption at rest and in transit
    • Employ fine-grained access controls and audit logs
    • Adhere to minimum necessary access and role-based permissions
    • Are deployable in private, on-premises or air-gapped environments for maximum control
Whether it’s for patient monitoring, secure communications, or health data storage — your privacy is protected by principle, not just paperwork.

THE BARTER BANK
Led by co-founder Julie Shifflet, the Vector Trust Barter Bank is a 21st-century revival of the Knights Templar model of mutual aid — a distributed, secure, and trusted repository of shared goods and family necessities.
Imagine a world where no child goes without a stroller, no winter passes without warm coats, and no family must start from scratch. Items flow between hands, not wallets.
Strollers, cribs, boots, books, tools — everything from the practical to the sentimental is tracked, preserved, and made available to members through voluntary exchange and mutual trust.
    • Zero monetary requirement
    • Fully tracked & auditable inventory
    • Encrypted ownership histories
    • Secured and administered locally through trusted nodes
What used to be waste is now wealth. What used to be forgotten is now sacred.
This is more than a sharing network. It’s a living legacy — stewarded, not consumed.

MENTAL HEALTH COOPERATIVE
In alignment with our vision of holistic sovereignty, Vector Trust will sponsor a Mental Health Cooperative — a peer-powered, HIPAA-compliant support structure where healing professionals, caregivers, and the underserved can gather in dignity and care.
This initiative will:
    • Provide telehealth and secure online spaces for therapy, coaching, and consultation
    • Invite licensed practitioners to volunteer or contract for care hours
    • Allow vetted caregivers to support one another and share burdens in real time
    • Be governed by a trusted lead clinician, selected by merit and readiness
We do not need to wait for a broken system to fix itself. We build our own platform. Quietly. Competently. Compassionately.
Build it, and they will come.
