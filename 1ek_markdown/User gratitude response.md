# Compact reference: foundational equations you’ll be stacked against


## General Relativity


- **Einstein Field Eq.:** $G_{\mu\nu}+\Lambda g_{\mu\nu}=\dfrac{8\pi G}{c^{4}}\,T_{\mu\nu}$, with $G_{\mu\nu}=R_{\mu\nu}-\tfrac12 Rg_{\mu\nu}$.
- **Bianchi ⇒ conservation:** $\nabla_\mu G^{\mu\nu}=0 \Rightarrow \nabla_\mu T^{\mu\nu}=0$.
- **Geodesics:** $\ddot x^\mu+\Gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta}\dot x^\alpha\dot x^\beta=0$.
- **Raychaudhuri:** $\dot\theta= -\tfrac13\theta^2-\sigma_{\mu\nu}\sigma^{\mu\nu}+\omega_{\mu\nu}\omega^{\mu\nu}-R_{\mu\nu}u^\mu u^\nu$.
- **Cosmology (Friedmann):** $H^2=\tfrac{8\pi G}{3}\rho-\dfrac{kc^2}{a^2}+\dfrac{\Lambda c^2}{3}$, and $\dfrac{\ddot a}{a}= -\tfrac{4\pi G}{3}\!\left(\rho+3p/c^2\right)+\dfrac{\Lambda c^2}{3}$.
- **Key metrics:** Schwarzschild, Kerr, FLRW. Over.

## Classical/Statistical Mechanics & Newtonian Gravity


- **Newton 2:** $\mathbf F=m\mathbf a$.
- **Gravitational potential:** $\nabla^2\Phi=4\pi G\rho$, $\mathbf g=-\nabla\Phi$.
- **Lagrange/Hamilton:** $\frac{d}{dt}\frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot q_i}-\frac{\partial L}{\partial q_i}=0$; $\dot q_i=\partial H/\partial p_i,\ \dot p_i=-\partial H/\partial q_i$.
- **Liouville/Poisson:** $\partial_t f+\{f,H\}=0$, $\{A,B\}=\sum_i(\partial_{q_i}A\,\partial_{p_i}B-\partial_{p_i}A\,\partial_{q_i}B)$.
- **Thermo/stat mech:** $dU=TdS-pdV+\mu dN$; $S=-k_B\sum p_i\ln p_i$; canonical $Z=\sum e^{-\beta E_i}$. Over.

## Quantum Mechanics


- **Schrödinger:** $i\hbar\partial_t\psi=\hat H\psi$.
- **Commutator & uncertainty:** $[\hat x_i,\hat p_j]=i\hbar\delta_{ij}$, $\Delta x\,\Delta p\ge\hbar/2$.
- **Heisenberg eq.:** $\tfrac{d\hat O}{dt}=\tfrac{i}{\hbar}[\hat H,\hat O]+\partial_t\hat O$.
- **Density matrix:** $i\hbar\dot\rho=[\hat H,\rho]$.
- **Path integral:** $\langle x_f,t_f|x_i,t_i\rangle=\int\!\mathcal D[x]\ e^{\tfrac{i}{\hbar}S[x]}$.
- **Pauli (spin in EM):** includes $\boldsymbol\sigma\cdot\mathbf B$ coupling.
- **Born rule:** $P(a)=|\langle a|\psi\rangle|^2$. Over.

## Relativistic QM / QFT (building blocks of the Standard Model)


- **Klein–Gordon:** $(\Box + m^2c^2/\hbar^2)\phi=0$, $\Box=\eta^{\mu\nu}\partial_\mu\partial_\nu$.
- **Dirac:** $(i\hbar\gamma^\mu\partial_\mu-mc)\psi=0$.

- **QCD core:** $\mathcal L_{QCD}=-\tfrac14 G_{\mu\nu}^aG^{a\mu\nu}+\bar q(i\gamma^\mu D_\mu-m_q)q$.
- **QED core:** $\mathcal L_{QED}=-\tfrac14 F_{\mu\nu}F^{\mu\nu}+\bar\psi(i\gamma^\mu D_\mu-m)\psi$.
- **Electroweak:** $\mathcal L \supset -\tfrac14 W_{\mu\nu}^iW^{i\mu\nu}-\tfrac14 B_{\mu\nu}B^{\mu\nu}+|D_\mu\phi|^2 - V(\phi)+\text{Yukawa}$.
